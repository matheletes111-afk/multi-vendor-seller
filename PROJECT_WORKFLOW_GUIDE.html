Project MEEEM Marketplace Quick User Guide

Easy Guide: Pick Your Panel

This page explains how the system works for non-technical users. Use it like a checklist: choose your role/panel on the left, then follow only the steps inside that panel.

Panels
Shopping (Public)
Shop, cart, and checkout
1 Open the Home page
Home shows categories and an “Explore products” grid.
2 Browse items
Use filters, then open product/service details.
3 Add to Cart
If the product has variants (like size/color), choose the right variant first.
4 Checkout and place order (COD)
Review order summary, select an address, and place the order.
5 Quick cart review before payment
Check the selected variant (size/color) and quantity. Remove or update items if needed.
6 Confirm you got an order result
After placing the order, you should see it in your customer order area (so you can track progress).
My Customer Account
Profile and order tracking
1 Open customer dashboard
Check your order activity.
2 Manage your profile
Update details from settings.
3 See your orders
Open your order history and track progress.
4 Open an order to see status
Use order details to understand where your order is (processing/shipped/delivered).
5 Update your profile for future orders
Keep address/profile details up to date so checkout stays easy.
If you can’t see cart buttons: confirm you are logged in as customer. Cart/checkout is hidden for seller/admin accounts.
Products
Create and manage your product catalog
1 Open product seller overview
Start here to see your products and actions.
2 View products list
Check what is currently available (and what needs changes).
3 Add a new product
Set category, images, variants (size/color), and stock quantity.
4 Preview your product page
Make sure images look correct and variant options appear properly.
5 Publish / save changes
After saving, confirm the product shows in your products list.
Subscription limits
Your plan controls what you can list
1 Open subscription plans
Current plan can restrict maximum number of products. (For testing, switching can be done without payment.)
2 Check your allowed limit
Look at how many products your current plan lets you have.
3 If you reach the limit, take action
Either upgrade your plan, or adjust existing products (edit/remove) to make space.
4 Test the rules (optional)
Try adding another product after switching plans to confirm restrictions work as expected.
Profile & settings
Keep your seller profile updated
1 Open profile / settings
2 Update your seller details
Keep your basic info and contact details correct.
3 Check your “ready to sell” settings
Make sure your account is set up so customers can order easily.
Promote your products (Seller Ads)
Request an ad to boost a specific product
1 Open promotion (ads) area
This is where you choose what to promote.
2 Select the product you want to promote
Pick from your available products.
3 Choose promotion timing
For testing, you can start quickly and see how it appears in the marketplace.
4 Submit the promotion request
Admin approves (or pauses) promotions.
5 Track the promotion status
Wait for approval, then watch when it becomes visible on the marketplace.
6 If rejected, fix and resubmit
When admin rejects, update your promotion and send it again with the same product.
7 Confirm it shows in the right place
Check the home/listing area where ads should appear during the active time.
After approval, your promoted product can show on the marketplace. When the time ends, the promotion stops automatically.
Services
Create and manage your service listings
1 Open service seller overview
See what services you have and what needs updates.
2 View services list
3 Add a new service
Set service type, base price/discount, images, and weekly availability (if needed).
4 Double-check schedule and availability
If availability is weekly, select correct days/times.
5 Save and confirm your listing
After saving, make sure it appears in your services list.
Subscription limits
Your plan controls what you can list
1 Open subscription plans
Current plan can restrict maximum number of services.
2 Check your allowed limit
See how many services you can list right now.
3 If you hit the limit, fix it
Upgrade your plan or edit/remove existing services to create space.
4 Verify restrictions after changes
Try creating a service again to confirm the limit enforcement works.
Profile & settings
Keep your seller profile updated
1 Open profile / settings
2 Update your service seller details
Keep contact and basic seller info correct.
3 Check your selling readiness
Make sure customers can book/order without confusion.
Promote your services (Seller Ads)
Request a promotion to boost visibility
1 Open promotion (ads) area
Choose what you want to promote.
2 Select the service to promote
Pick from your service listings.
3 Choose promotion timing
For testing, start quickly and see where it appears.
4 Submit promotion request
Admin approves (or pauses) promotions.
5 Track the promotion status
Wait for approval, then watch when it becomes visible on the marketplace.
6 If rejected, fix and resubmit
Adjust the promotion request and submit again.
7 Confirm the ad location
Check the home/listing area where promoted services are supposed to appear.
After approval, your promoted service can show on the marketplace. When the time ends, the promotion stops automatically.
Manage orders
From new order to completed order
1 Open the order list
Find the order that needs attention.
2 Check order details
Verify customer info, items, totals, and order method (COD).
3 Update the order progress
Move the order through the correct next step (packed/shipped/delivered or cancelled).
4 Resolve issues
If something is wrong, cancel or adjust the order as needed and note the reason.
5 Confirm completion
When all steps are done, make sure the order status updates correctly.
6 Keep a clear record
Write a short reason for the change so the seller and customer understand what happened.
Manage sellers
Keep sellers active and trustworthy
1 Open the sellers list
Review each seller account.
2 Check seller readiness
Look at plan status and whether their promotions are approved or paused.
3 Activate or deactivate
If a seller should stop selling, deactivate them and inform them.
4 Review seller promotions (if any)
Ensure promoted products/services follow the rules and display correctly.
5 Check plan limits match reality
Verify that listing counts match what the plan allows.
6 Communicate next actions
If something needs change, tell the seller what to do.
Manage subscriptions
Control listing limits by plan
1 Open subscription overview
See which sellers are on which plan.
2 Apply plan changes
Update plan for testing or when a seller upgrades.
3 Confirm limits are enforced
Make sure max products/services limits match the plan rules.
4 Re-check after saving
Refresh and confirm the new limits are reflected.
5 Document what changed
Keep notes for yourself so you can explain decisions later.
Manage categories & marketplace content
Keep the marketplace organized
1 Edit categories
Maintain product categories and service categories.
2 Manage banners and ads
Update what appears on the home and listing screens.
3 Clean up old or wrong category mappings
Make sure products and services appear under the right category.
4 Check “what customers see”
Open the home/browse screens and verify the marketplace looks correct.
5 Keep listings consistent
Use a simple rule: if something looks confusing, fix it now.
Manage seller promotions (Seller Ads)
Approve and control promoted products/services
1 Open promotion requests
See what sellers have requested to promote.
2 Review what will be shown
Check the promoted item, promotion timing, and the seller plan.
3 Approve, reject, or pause
Approve to publish the promotion, or pause/reject if needed. If rejected, add a short reason for the seller.
4 Monitor during active time
When the time ends, the promotion should stop automatically.
5 Record your decision + reason
Approve, reject, or pause with a short explanation (especially if rejected).
6 Verify start/end time behavior
Confirm the promotion becomes active on time and stops at the correct end time.
This is the admin side of “seller ad management” (promoting a product/service by request).

Common Tips (Everyone)

Quick reminders
1 Make sure you are logged into the correct role
Cart + checkout are customer-only. Seller/admin accounts won’t see customer cart buttons.
2 For products, variants matter
If a product has variants (size/color), you must select a valid variant before “Add to Cart” from the product detail page.
3 For quick testing (subscription)
Subscription plan buttons are meant to be used for testing restrictions today. Payment flow can be added later.
